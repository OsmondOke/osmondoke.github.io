As much as we enjoy the Telecommunication services provided to us, we always want “better”; be it speed, bonus features,

add-ons, pricing, etc, and because there are many other companies providing the same services, what would

make a customer stay with their service provider, and not drive them to cancel their subscriptions, and leave

for their subjective better (Churn)? This is where the problem arises. In this project, my aim was to understand

the patterns in the customers that churned, in order to make accurate predictions about what users would be most

likely to churn, and in turn try to prevent it when possible.
